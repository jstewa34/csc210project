Fantasy Survivor

Survivor; social experiment, strategy game, and the ultimate reality competition show that has been airing since the dawn of the 21st century, season after season, drawing in millions of viewers and producing many self-proclaimed superfans who have even gone on to compete on the show.

How does it work?

The game ever evolves and has spawned many iterations of itself in countries across the globe, all guided by the provocative premise of castaways being voted out one by one until only one remains and claims the large monetary prize and exclusive, legendary title of Sole Survivor.

To add an extra layer of suspense and fun to watching the show and some friendly competition amongst fellow fans, we plan to create a website that will allow users to form and participate in a Survivor fantasy league, àlà Fantasy Football and other Fantasy leagues that are quite popular among sports fans. A user will have their own account which they will log in to. At the start of the show season, there will be a draft where the user and fellow league members will select castaways on the season for their fantasy team. Points will be awarded or deducted based on what one’s players do in the show each episode (e.g. win individual immunity? +50 pts, get blindsided!? -100 pts). Point totals for each player will accumulate throughout episodes and be added accordingly to the point total of the team they belong to, until the end of the season where the user in the Fantasy League who has managed to accrue the most points for their team shall be declared the victor!
